About Me

Hello! I'm Niraj Kumar, a second-year B.Tech Computer Science student at Lovely Professional University. My passion for technology and data-driven insights has led me to dive deep into the world of Data Science, where I'm constantly exploring new ways to extract meaningful information from complex datasets.

Since beginning my journey in computer science, I've built a strong foundation in programming, statistics, and analytical thinking. My coursework has provided me with a solid understanding of algorithms, data structures, and machine learning principles. However, it was my curiosity about how data shapes the world that truly sparked my interest and inspired me to specialize in Data Science.

Over the past year, I've been sharpening my skills in Python, SQL, and data visualization tools like Tableau and Power BI, along with libraries such as Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, and Matplotlib. I enjoy uncovering patterns in data and solving real-world problems through models and storytelling. Some of my projects include building a movie recommendation system, analyzing stock market trends, and predicting student performance using machine learning.

In addition to my technical skills, I enjoy collaborating with others and learning from diverse perspectives. Working on team projects and participating in data challenges and hackathons have taught me the importance of communication, adaptability, and continuous learning.

When I'm not analyzing data, you can find me exploring tech blogs, playing chess, or reading books. These activities keep me refreshed and often inspire new ideas for my projects.

I'm excited to continue my journey in Data Science and contribute to impactful projects that drive informed decisions and innovation. Feel free to explore my portfolio and reach out if you'd like to connect or collaborate on future projects. You can contact me via Email or connect with me on LinkedIn.
